Travel Notes

flight_takeoff
Getting Here

O.R. Tambo International Airport is the nearest major airport, approximately 45–60 minutes from Pretoria, depending on traffic. We recommend renting a car or booking a private transfer.

thermostat
Spring Weather

September brings beautiful blooms, warmth and a refreshing breeze. However, evenings can be crisp; a light wrap or blazer is recommended for the evening reception.

local_taxi
Local Transport

While a shuttle has been arranged to and from the venue, we cannot accomodate for everyone. Guests are encouraged to use their personal vehicles to attend the ceremony for ease of commuting to your preferred accommodation

wine_bar
Local Attractions

You are free and welcome to visit any and all other attractions in Pretoria and the surrounding areas. We recommend the Pretoria Botanical Gardens.
