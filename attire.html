Dress Code Guide

The Spring Palette

A celebration of lush tones and elegant textures. We invite our guests to embrace the vibrant energy of the season through our curated color story.

The mood is Soft Spring Luxe.

Guests are kindly encouraged to wear elegant spring tones such as sage, olive, eucalyptus, taupe, stone, warm grey, mocha, butter yellow and muted pastels like soft lavender.

Or

Spring Bright (Elegant & Refined)

Guests are also encouraged to wear fresh spring brights such as cornflower or soft royal blue, apple, lime or pistachio green, butter or marigold yellow, and soft coral tones, paired with warm neutrals like taupe, stone, camel, or warm grey.

Aesthetic Preservation

To preserve the wedding aesthetic, guests are kindly asked to avoid and NOT WEAR:

  • Pink
  • Champagne
  • Beige


  • White
  • Black
  • Neon


  • Overly shiny or embellished fabrics
