Cookie Policy

Babo & Keagile

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website may place on your device when you visit. They are widely used to make sites work more efficiently and to give the site owner useful information.

How we use cookies

This website uses only essential cookies that are necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising, analytics, or third‑party tracking cookies.

Essential cookies we use

  • Session cookie — So we can remember that you have been verified as a guest and keep you signed in as you move between pages. This cookie is removed when you close your browser or when you revoke consent.
  • Cookie consent record — A cookie that records that you have accepted the use of cookies, so we can demonstrate that consent was given. It is stored for up to one year.

Your choice

You may withdraw your consent to the use of cookies at any time. If you do, we will stop using cookies for you and you will lose access to the private areas of this site. You will need to go through the guest check at the gate again if you wish to access the site later.

Revoke cookie consent

Click the button below to withdraw your consent. We will delete our cookies and end your session. You will be taken back to the gate and will need to enter your name again and accept cookies to continue.
